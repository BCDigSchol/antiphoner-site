How to use the manuscript viewer

The manuscript viewer enables you to view a digitized image of each folio in the Franciscan antiphoner (left side) with the corresponding incipit text (right side). Zoom in and out as well as a full-screen mode is also available.
As you scroll through the images, corresponding incipit texts will appear in the right side of the viewer.




When you click on one of the incipit texts, it will display corresponding data for each incipit (when available), including Feast, Office, Genre, and Mode. It will also include the musical incipit displayed using Volpiano font. For additional definitions and descriptions, visit the "Description of Fields and Their Contents" page in CANTUS.




    To search the manuscript, click on the "Search" tab. You can do the following searches:
  • Full Text - type in the text that might be found in the incipits or data associated with the manuscript. This is limited to the incipits and data that has been encoded for this project.


  • Drop-down menu - select from Genre, Feast, Office, or Mode.


  • Volpiano - type lower-case ASCII letters to represent the pitches of a melody.
